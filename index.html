Future Planning Idea: Portable Designer

Overview/Content

Many architecture designs on computers. But what if they have to travel. I was having this idea as a tape measure, where the ruler can be pulled out so when connecting to the technology, we can design it into when pressing a button, the screen will be able to be move/go back. Architecture is also designing and making blueprints on paper, but if they lose it and use too much paper, it is bad for the environment. But what happens if they need to travel, they won’t be able to bring a whole computer with them. So let's see my idea.

  • So I was thinking, if we can make a portable and foldable/roll up technology, it would be much easier and design everywhere they want.
  • They can open/pull out an electronic scroll and start designing and pull the pen out of the side corner.
  • It will be waterproof, so the architecture will be able to be not worried about the design getting wet like how in paper
  • Like making a screen that is foldable and flexible.
  • On the side of the screen, it can help calculate the ratio and the measurements to make sure that it fits inside the right amount of land/ space that you have. Also, calculate the proportional. For instance, there will be a key such as 1cm = 5 m
    • For this, the architecture can choose what types of unis or measurments the architect wants.
  • After scrolling up, the final product wil be 13 inches in height. After pulling out the screen, the width is 25 inches.

Tech careers in the field of architecture

Architecture Offices

Description: They plan, design, and oversees the complex project.

Interior designs

Description: They make interior spaces functional, safe, and beautiful for almost every type of building

Landscape architecture

Description: They design attractive and functional public parks, gardens, playgrounds, residential areas, college campuses, and public spaces.

Urban Planners

Description: They develop land use plans and programs that help create communities and revitalize physical facilities in towns, cities, counties, and metropolitan areas.

Engineers & Consultants

Description: They provide engineering advice and technical solutions for projects.

Project Managers

Description: They plan, organize, and direct the completion of specific projects for an organization while they ensure these projects are on time, on budget.

Construction companies

Description: Construction management companies are the boots on the ground, overseeing every aspect of a construction project.

Data scientist and analyst

Description: Data scientist works on new ways of capturing and analyzing data to be used by the analysts

3D modeling or visualization

Description: They transform an idea or 2D design into an easy-to-understand, high-impact image that enables clients to view and understand their design in a more tangible way.
